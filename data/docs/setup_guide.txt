To get started, create a TechEase account using your email address.
After signup, verify your email to activate the account.
Log in and open Settings to configure your workspace name and timezone.
Invite team members by email from the Team section.
Connect at least one integration (for example Slack or Google Workspace) if needed.
Create your first project and define the key workflow steps.
Enable notifications to receive updates when tasks change status.
Use the Help Center inside the app for common setup questions.
If you see errors during setup, restart the browser and try again.
If the issue continues, contact support with a screenshot and the time of the error.
